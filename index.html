About Me

Hi, I'm Your Dog's New Best Ally 🐾

I'm a certified dog behaviorist with over 8 years of experience helping dogs and their families build deeper, more trusting relationships. I specialize in fear, anxiety, reactivity, and aggression — using evidence-based, force-free methods that respect both dog and human.

My approach isn't about commands and compliance — it's about truly understanding your dog's emotional world. When your dog feels safe and understood, everything else follows naturally.

I work from the heart of your home, either in-person or via telehealth, because that's where real behavior change happens.

🎓 CAAB Certified 💙 Fear-Free Pro 🐾 IAABC Member 📚 B.Sc. Animal Behavior 🌿 Force-Free Only

Policies & Agreements

Transparency You Can Trust

I believe in being clear and upfront about how we work together. Please take a moment to read our policies before your first session.

📋 Liability Waiver

By booking a session with The Dog Therapist, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

  • Dog behavior is unpredictable. I will take every reasonable precaution, but cannot guarantee specific outcomes.
  • You are responsible for the safety and behavior of your dog at all times during and after our sessions.
  • The Dog Therapist is not liable for any injury to persons or property caused by your dog.
  • Recommendations provided are educational in nature and do not replace veterinary medical advice.
  • A digital signature on your intake form constitutes your agreement to this waiver.

🎥 Filming & Content Policy

I create educational content about dog behavior for social media and online platforms. Here's what you need to know:

  • Filming during sessions is entirely optional and requires your explicit written consent.
  • You may opt out of filming at any time — on the intake form, or verbally before your session.
  • If you consent, footage may be edited and shared on Instagram, YouTube, or TikTok for educational purposes.
  • You will never be identified by full name without additional written consent.
  • You may request footage removal at any time by contacting hello@thedogtherapist.net.

💰 Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • 48+ hours notice: Full refund or free reschedule.
  • 24–48 hours notice: 50% credit toward a future session.
  • Less than 24 hours: Session fee is forfeited, except in documented emergencies.
  • Packages: Pro-rated refunds available within the first 14 days.
  • All cancellations must be made via email or text message.

🔒 Privacy & Data Policy

Your privacy matters deeply to me.

  • All personal information collected via intake forms is stored securely in encrypted cloud storage.
  • Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties without your consent.
  • Intake forms and session notes are retained for 3 years for professional records purposes.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time.
  • This website uses cookies for analytics only. No personal data is collected via cookies.

By booking a session, you acknowledge having read and agreed to these policies. A copy of all agreements is included in your intake form for your records.
